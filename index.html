Artificial Intelligence in Romania

Romanian AI community

Our aim: support the Romanian Artificial Intelligence (AI) community to grow and develop AI technologies. 

Vision: use AI to enhance Romanians' lives.

Join us on airomania slack channel for discussions and collaborations around AI research, AI education, and more.

Follow us on AIRomania Twitter!

Check out the projects that we are currently developing.    

NEWS: Our project, AI for highschool course, was officially approved by the Ministry of Education to be included as optional discipline in Romanian highschools. Course materials and curriculum available at https://www.pyml.ro/. We are currently running a training course for highschool teachers to teach the course.
